### Critical Discovery: Data Doesn't Exist
After extensive testing, discovered fundamental limitation:
- Real customers seeking services don't post emails publicly
- They use Upwork/Fiverr, say "DM me", or ask privately
- Suleman wants 15K leads/month with emails — NOT achievable via free scraping
- Realistic: 50-200 leads/month, most without direct email

### Types of Wrong Results Filtered Out

3. **Service providers** (developers advertising themselves)
4. **Companies** (publishers, agencies, studios)
5. **Old posts** (timeframe filter added)
